Dining

Lavender Ridge understands that as memory impairments progress the dining experience can become confusing and frustrating. We offer three home cooked meals and can accommodate most special diets. All of our meals are prepared on site by our experienced staff and served family style. We also encourage snacks and hydration throughout the day.

The dining room overlooks the garden and patio area providing residents a home-like view while dining. The staff of Lavender Ridge dines with the residents at meal times to provide conversation, encouragement, and assistance with a non-threatening approach.

Guests are encouraged to dine with their loved ones. There is a minimal charge of $5.00 per person. We ask that guests contact us 24 hours in advance so the staff can make proper accommodations.
